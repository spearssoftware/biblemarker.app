Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Spears Software ("we," "our," or "us") operates the BibleMarker mobile application and website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our BibleMarker.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

The BibleMarker operates primarily offline and stores data locally on your device. We may collect the following information:

  • Bible API Keys: Premium translations (ESV, LEB, Darby, YLT, The Emphasized Bible) require you to provide your own API keys obtained directly from the respective API providers (Crossway for ESV, Faithlife/Logos for Biblia API translations). Spears Software does not provide API keys. These API keys are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • User Content: All annotations, highlights, notes, keywords, studies, and other content you create in the app are stored locally on your device using IndexedDB.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Bible Text Caching: The app may cache Bible chapters you access for offline reading. This data is stored locally on your device only.
  • Usage Data: The app may store local usage preferences and settings on your device.

1.3 Third-Party Services

The app integrates with the following third-party Bible API services. Important: Premium translations require your own API keys.

  • getBible API: Free Bible translation service providing 100+ public domain translations including KJV, ASV, WEB, and many others. No API key required. See their privacy policy for information about their data practices.
  • Biblia API (Faithlife/Logos): Provides LEB (Lexham English Bible), Darby Bible, YLT (Young's Literal Translation), and The Emphasized Bible. Requires your own API key obtained directly from Faithlife/Logos. Subject to their terms and privacy policy.
  • ESV API (Crossway): Provides ESV (English Standard Version) translation. Requires your own API key obtained directly from Crossway and is subject to their terms and privacy policy, including noncommercial use requirements.

When you use these services, your requests are made directly from your device to the respective API providers using your own API keys. We do not intercept, store, or have access to these API requests. You are responsible for complying with all terms of service of API providers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since the app primarily operates offline and stores data locally:

  • All your annotations, notes, and study data remain on your device
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal Bible study content to our servers
  • API keys you provide are stored locally and only used to make direct API calls from your device to Bible translation services
  • We do not access or share your study data

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All user data (annotations, notes, keywords, studies, API keys, cached Bible chapters) is stored locally on your device using browser IndexedDB or similar local storage mechanisms.
  • No Cloud Sync: Currently, the app does not sync data to cloud servers. All data remains on your device.
  • Security: While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties because:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not have access to your data
  • We do not operate servers that receive or store user data

However, when you use Bible API services (getBible, Biblia, ESV API), your requests are made directly from your device to those services, and they may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access Your Data: All your data is stored on your device and accessible within the app.
  • Delete Your Data: You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app, which will remove all locally stored data. You can also clear specific annotations or studies within the app.
  • API Key Management: You can add, modify, or remove Bible API keys at any time through the app settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have certain rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and the right to request deletion of your personal information. However, since our app stores all data locally on your device and we do not collect or store personal information on our servers, most CCPA rights do not apply to the BibleMarker.

9. GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights. Since our app does not collect or process personal data on our servers, your data protection rights are primarily exercised through your control over the data stored on your device.
