BibleMarker

Deep, meaningful Bible study with powerful annotation tools, keyword tracking, and multi-translation support

macOS • Windows • Linux • iOS

Mark What Matters

Key words are the foundation of inductive study — authors repeat them to drive their point home. Define a keyword once with a color and symbol, and BibleMarker tracks every occurrence across the text automatically. Select any word to apply an existing keyword, create a new one, or look up the original Hebrew or Greek via Strong’s Numbers.

Record What You See

The Observe panel is your structured notebook for the text. Make lists of what you learn about key people or themes, answer the 5 W’s & H (Who, What, When, Where, Why, How), note contrasts and comparisons, tag time expressions, and track places and people — all anchored to the passage you’re studying.

See the Bigger Picture

The Analyze panel turns your observations into insight. View time expressions on a timeline, explore geographic locations on an interactive map, and work through interpretation and application worksheets. Chapter summaries, book overviews, and theme tracking give structure to a full study.
